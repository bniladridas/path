our development philosophy

every update to path is guided by our four pillars:

  • authentic discovery - no manipulation, just honest responses
  • human longing - honoring the deeper needs behind every search
  • time is precious - respecting your attention and time
  • coming home - helping you find your way back to yourself

what's next

future updates will continue to focus on:

  • improving response quality and relevance
  • enhancing the user interface and experience
  • adding new transparency and customization features
  • maintaining our commitment to privacy and honesty

feedback and suggestions

staying updated

we'll update this page with each new version of path. significant changes will also be reflected in our terms of use and privacy policy as needed.
