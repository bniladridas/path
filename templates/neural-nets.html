understanding the neural networks that power harper:
component details
model architecture google gemini 2.0 flash - transformer-based multimodal neural network
context window 1 million input tokens, 8K output - massive context understanding
training approach reinforcement learning from human feedback (rlhf)
capabilities text generation, reasoning, analysis, creative writing
optimization flash variant optimized for speed and efficiency
safety measures constitutional ai, content filtering, bias mitigation
harper uses these neural networks not to replace human creativity, but to help you discover media that genuinely resonates with your soul. the ai processes your queries through billions of parameters trained on diverse media knowledge, then generates responses focused on authentic discovery rather than algorithmic manipulation. every response you receive has traveled through layers of neural attention mechanisms, each one designed to understand context, meaning, and your underlying intent to find something meaningful.
