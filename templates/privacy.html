harper privacy policy

last updated: 2025-05-27

1. our commitment to privacy

harper is built on the principle of radical transparency and user respect. this privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our ai-powered media exploration service.

2. information we collect

collection type information collected
automatically collected connection protocol information (http/https, hostname, port), browser information (user agent, language, platform), basic usage analytics (page views, feature usage)
voluntarily provided verification responses (human verification only), gmail domain verification (domain only, not full address), conversation inputs and queries

3. how we use your information

we use collected information solely to:

purpose description
service provision provide and improve the harper service
user verification verify user authenticity (human verification)
ai processing process ai queries through google's gemini api
security ensure service security and prevent abuse
analytics analyze usage patterns for service improvement

4. conversation privacy

harper operates with complete conversation privacy:

privacy feature description
no message history we do not show the ai your previous conversations or message history
fresh interactions each conversation with harper starts completely clean
real-time processing your queries are processed immediately and not stored
session-based only conversations exist only during your active session
no conversation logs we don't maintain records of what you've discussed

5. what we don't do

harper is committed to privacy protection. we never:

privacy commitment what we never do
data storage store your conversation history or queries
email privacy save gmail addresses or personal email information
data sharing sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
advertising use your data for advertising or marketing
cross-site tracking track you across other websites or services
profiling create user profiles or behavioral tracking

6. code of conduct

your use of harper is also governed by our terms of use, which includes our code of conduct outlining expected behavior and community guidelines for respectful usage of our service.

7. third-party services

google gemini api:

your queries are processed through google's gemini 2.0 flash api. under our paid tier usage, google does not use your conversations to improve their products. your data is protected by google's enterprise-grade security.

gmail verification:

we only verify that an email address ends with "@gmail.com" for automatic verification bypass. we never store, access, or process the actual email address.

8. data retention

harper follows a minimal data retention policy:

data type retention policy
conversation data not stored (processed in real-time only)
verification data cleared after session
analytics data aggregated and anonymized, retained for service improvement
technical logs retained for 30 days for security purposes

9. security measures

we implement industry-standard security measures including:

security measure implementation
encryption https encryption for all data transmission
api security secure api connections to google services
maintenance regular security audits and updates
data minimization minimal data collection and storage

10. your rights

you have the right to:

user right description
service control stop using harper at any time
data inquiry request information about data we may have
privacy concerns report privacy concerns through our interface
anonymous usage use harper without creating accounts or profiles

11. children's privacy

harper is not intended for children under 13. we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. if we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

12. international users

harper is operated from the united states. by using harper, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the united states, subject to this privacy policy.

13. changes to this policy

we may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. we will notify users of significant changes through the application interface. you can view our complete changelog and version history on our updates page.

14. contact us

for privacy questions or concerns, please contact us through the harper interface or use the three-dot menu options.
